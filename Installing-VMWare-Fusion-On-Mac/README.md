# Installing VMWare Fusion On Mac
#### Download the VMware Fusion
* Access the [link](https://www.vmware.com/products/desktop-hypervisor/workstation-and-fusion) to download the VMware Fusion.

<img width="687" height="272" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6807d8a6-30f8-4796-b454-cf0b2f037e80" />

* Click the “Download Fusion or Workstation”. After you click it, you’ll be routed to Broadcom’s login page.

<img width="687" height="323" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ed925695-d349-4906-be00-a57307412fcc" />

* If you already have an account with Broadcom, then you can just log in directly to their website. Otherwise, click the "Login" button on the upper right side of the website, and then click "Register". Just follow the instructions on how to register your account.

<img width="687" height="253" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bb73287a-e65b-4ecd-a0d9-7ac78ad3650c" />

* When you’re logged in, navigate to “My Downloads” and then search for VMware Fusion in the search bar on the upper right side.

<img width="687" height="337" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/62707a31-47f1-4868-903f-8301e756b1c2" />

* Choose the latest release of the update which is 13.6.2.
* After you click it, you will be routed to the download section.

<img width="670" height="214" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/45e104e7-6d0e-42ed-af96-a81dca3e7c77" />

* Click the download icon to download the software.
* After you download, just click the file and the installer window will pop up.

  <img width="697" height="524" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3cad727c-2ebe-49d2-884b-f7d3dc77e690" />

* Double-click the VMware Fusion logo to install it.
* After clicking, you will be asked, “VMware Fusion is an app downloaded from the internet. Are you sure you want to open it?”

<img width="697" height="448" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9a0a24b5-0a91-4310-8341-e82bd3f6c6da" />

* Click “Open”, and you should provide your password on your host machine to continue the setup of your software application.

  <img width="697" height="695" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7f987bf4-dbb2-4791-92a0-5db27f747676" />

* After providing the password, you need to read and agree to their Terms & Conditions in order to use VMware Fusion 13.

<img width="683" height="436" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2ab69275-e5ab-4379-b324-d568c3873ffd" />

* After you agree to their Terms and Conditions, you are done.
